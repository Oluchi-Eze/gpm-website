GPM Vision & Mission

Mark 16: 15-16 “15 He said to them, “Go into all the world and preach the gospel to all creation.16 Whoever believes and is baptised will be saved, but whoever does not believe will be condemned.”

Our Vision

Our vision is to impact seven billion souls with the culture of God’s kingdom and joy as commissioned by Christ Jesus of Nazareth.

Our Mission

Education, Training and communication culture of health and peace in accordance with the teaching of Christ Jesus of Nazareth about the kingdom of God.
