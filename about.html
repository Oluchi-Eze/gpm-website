About Us

God Peace Ministry seeks The Kingdom of God (Matthew 6:31). The institution follows Christ Jesus of Nazareth's teaching. It is a non-denominational congregation. We educate on the Kingdom of God's life.

ONE CONGREGATION
DIFFERENT PEOPLE

Have you pondered on this question: what is most important for you? What do you want to do with your own and only precious life—a life that has no duplicate? This question is very personal and demands a personal answer.

TAKE YOUR
NEXT STEP

Seeking the right answer is our commitment. The right answer calms your soul, heart, mind and gives you clarity. Join us now. We have different opportunities for the seeker both in learning and serving.

YOU MAKE
A DIFFERENCE.

The only lasting answer to your worry and fear is to find GOD. The Joy and Peace you receive make a difference for you — and you make a difference for others.

Our Leadership

Your talent and gift give you access to leadership service. Members are encouraged to discover their areas of leadership. Character and habits worthy of leadership are considered.

Madelon Grant

Stewardship

Madelon Grant is 42 years old and serves God Peace Ministry as a steward. She uses her organisational skills to provide the basic practical issues that are involved in being a healthy, stable church.

Aurelia Osunwoke

Youth Leader

Aurelia Osunwoke is 24 years old. She was born and raised in Amsterdam, is a trained recruiter and works as an administrative staff. She has a mixed Nigerian and Surinamese background.

Prince Olikeze

Music

Prince Olikeze leads the worship at God Peace Ministry. He uses his personal experiences of hardship and God's power to deliver and protect us from harm to encourage others with similar experiences.

Holiness

Women Ministry

Holiness is a dedicated prayer and worshipper with a special calling to bring spiritually battered and broken women into the healing light of Christ. She is mother of two children and within God Peace Ministry she is leading the women ministry.

Godian Ejiogu

General Overseer

Godian Ejiogu is a spiritual caregiver, theologian, and pastoral worker specializing in culturally sensitive care. He promotes peace and dignity, and guides people to find spiritual clarity and joy. He leads God Peace Ministry and other community-focused organizations.
